About me and my coutry

I'm from Macedonia. I have 30 years, I was studying the Department of Electrical Engineering and Automatic Control. I was born in Prilep, which is far larger city then Radovish, but my job made ​​me to move here.

Macedonia is a country located in the central Balkan in Southeast Europe. It is one of the successor states of the former Yugoslavia, from which it declared independence in 1991. It became a member of the United Nations in 1993 but, as a result of a dispute with Greece over its name, it was admitted under the provisional reference of the former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia.

The Republic of Macedonia is bordered by Kosovo to the northwest, Serbia to the north, Bulgaria to the east, Greece to the south, and Albania to the west. The country's capital is Skopje, with 506,926 inhabitants according to the 2002 census. Other cities include Bitola, Kumanovo, Prilep, Tetovo, Ohrid, Veles, Štip, Kočani, Gostivar, Kavadarci, Strumica.... It has more than 50 lakes and sixteen mountains higher than 2,000 m.

Macedonia is a member of the UN and the Council of Europe. Since December 2005 it has also been a candidate for joining the European Union and has applied for NATO membership.
